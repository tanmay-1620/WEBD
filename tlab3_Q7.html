Favourite Team

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded on March 6, 1902, it is one of the most successful clubs in the world, with a record 13 UEFA Champions League titles and numerous domestic league titles.

The club's home stadium is the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, which has a capacity of over 81,000 spectators. Real Madrid's traditional home kit is all-white, and the club's crest features a crown, symbolizing its status as the "king of clubs."

Real Madrid has a long-standing rivalry with Barcelona, known as "El Clásico," which is one of the most famous and fiercely contested matches in football.

Some of the greatest footballers in history, including Alfredo Di Stéfano, Ferenc Puskás, Zinedine Zidane, Cristiano Ronaldo, and many others, have played for Real Madrid.
